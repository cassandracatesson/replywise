Frequently Asked Questions

How does ReplyWise score my emails? +
ReplyWise combines real reply outcome data with neuroscience research on how the brain processes text. We score across dimensions like personalization, clarity, emotional salience, and cognitive engagement - calibrated using brain imaging studies on language comprehension. It can also write emails for you, optimized to score high.
What email providers does ReplyWise work with? +
ReplyWise works with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail, and ProtonMail. We're constantly adding support for more providers. The free plan includes Gmail and Outlook, while Pro and Teams plans unlock all supported providers.
How do you track replies? +
ReplyWise automatically detects replies to your emails using thread detection and smart parsing. We use unique markers to match replies to the original emails you sent. All without storing your email content.
Is my data really private? +
Absolutely. We never store your actual email content. We only analyze patterns in real-time and store anonymized metadata. Your emails never leave your mailbox. We're GDPR and CCPA compliant, and we never sell your data.
Can I use ReplyWise on multiple devices? +
Yes! Your ReplyWise account syncs across all your devices. Install the Chrome extension on any device, sign in with your account, and your data and settings are available everywhere.
